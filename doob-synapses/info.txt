THE ONE-LINE IDEA
-----------------
Cast per-synapse consolidation and plasticity as two regimes of ONE Doob-h-transform / barrier-conditioned
diffusion: condition each weight's stochastic dynamics on the event of NOT crossing its memory-critical
level, so a synapse that encodes an important memory has its OWN intrinsic analog noise steered to keep it
there. On neuromorphic silicon the chip's intrinsic device noise, normally an accuracy tax, becomes the
consolidation mechanism -- a continual-learning dividend a GPU has to PAY to fake.

WHAT THE KILL-TEST KILLED, AND WHAT SURVIVED (read first -- these are hard constraints, not suggestions)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The mechanism has THREE parts. The kill-test surrendered one and kept two, and the paper lives or dies on
owning that split honestly.

- SURRENDER THE DRIFT (do NOT claim it as novel). The closed-form consolidation drift -s_i*(w_i - L_i)
  (uncertainty-scaled pull toward an anchor) is a re-derivation, and the kill-test PROVED it, not just
  "structurally similar":
    * OUA -- Ornstein-Uhlenbeck Adaptation (Garcia Fernandez, Ahmad, van Gerven, arXiv:2410.13563, Oct
      2024): learning as a mean-reverting OU diffusion d(theta)=lambda(mu-theta)dt + Sigma dW -- the EXACT
      anchored-diffusion drift, as a stochastic-process learning rule.
    * MESU (Nat Commun 2025 / PMC12575836, Eq. 11): the uncertainty(variance)-scaled anchor pull toward
      mu_prior, verbatim, as a Bayesian-continual-learning update.
    * EWC (Kirkpatrick et al. 2017): the Fisher-weighted quadratic anchor, the regularization ancestor.
  State in the paper, UP FRONT, that the drift is a known limit of OUA/MESU/EWC. Owning this as identity
  (not dressing it as novelty) is what keeps the paper credible; a reviewer WILL know OUA.

- KEEP (the two-piece conjunction that is the entire contribution):
  (a) THE DOOB / BARRIER-CONDITIONING FRAMING AS A SYNAPTIC RULE. Casting per-synapse consolidation as a
      Doob-h-transform / first-passage-conditioned diffusion (condition on not crossing the memory-critical
      barrier) is genuinely unclaimed -- every Doob-h-transform use found is generative modeling /
      Schrodinger bridge / transition-path / filtering; ZERO synaptic/plasticity/CL use. This absence IS
      the moat; cite the generative-Doob corpus to prove it.
  (b) THE FALSIFIABLE HARDWARE CURVE: "increasing the chip's OWN intrinsic analog noise NON-MONOTONICALLY
      IMPROVES retention across SEQUENTIAL tasks (an inverted-U optimum)" -- measured on real BSS-2 silicon.
      No candidate claims this (checked OUA, MESU, EWC, Kolesnikov-Semenova 2504.13778 = single-task
      test-noise robustness, Shaham 2022 = replay/noise-as-harmful/monotonic, NVRM/ANV = injected GPU
      weight noise for scale-robustness). This is the LOAD-BEARING result.

  THE WHOLE PAPER RESTS ON THE CONJUNCTION (a) AND (b). Absent (a) it is OUA/MESU. Absent (b) it is
  repackaged Bayesian-online CL. If the noise-helps-retention curve does not materialize, the mechanism
  reduces to OUA/MESU/EWC and THE PAPER HAS NO CORE -- so the curve is not a nice-to-have, it is the thesis.

EXPERIMENT LADDER (gated; the FIRST gate is a one-afternoon go/no-go for the whole project)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
E0. THE AFTERNOON FALSIFIER (GATE F -- run this BEFORE writing anything else). Pure Euler-Maruyama
    simulation on the 3080, hours not days: a small continual-learning testbed (Split-MNIST single head, or
    the Yin-Yang dataset; 3-5 sequential tasks). Implement the barrier-conditioned diffusion per weight
    (drift toward the anchor + intrinsic noise term conditioned on the memory barrier). SWEEP the intrinsic
    noise level from 0 upward; PLOT retention (mean accuracy on past tasks) vs noise level, >=5 seeds,
    mean+-sd. GATE F: a NON-MONOTONIC 'more noise -> better retention' curve (an inverted-U with a
    retention optimum at noise > 0) that the matched OU/EWC/MESU anchors WITHOUT the barrier-conditioning
    do NOT produce (run them as the control on the same testbed).
    - If the inverted-U appears: GO. This is the thesis; everything downstream confirms + scales it.
    - If retention is flat or monotonically-decreasing in noise: the mechanism reduces to OUA/MESU/EWC.
      STOP. Report the honest negative (an afternoon spent) -- and this project is dead; the companion swing
      (Selection-corrected data amplification, E:/GitHub/Research/selection-amplify) runs independently, and
      the ready backup swing is Kac-Rice Informative-Crossing Halting (GREEN_CAUTIOUS, not yet written).
E1. MECHANISM ISOLATION (sim). Show the effect is the BARRIER-CONDITIONING, not just any noise: ablate the
    conditioning (plain OU + unconditioned noise) -> curve should flatten; vary the barrier level L_i and
    the conditioning strength -> retention optimum should track them. Establish that noise HELPS only when
    steered by the not-crossing condition.
E2. REAL BSS-2 SILICON (the moat; the result a GPU-only researcher cannot produce). Port the rule to
    BrainScaleS-2, de-risked by the author's ALREADY-VALIDATED intrinsic-noise node-perturbation pipeline
    (0.90-0.95). Use the chip's OWN intrinsic analog noise as the diffusion term; tune the effective noise
    via the knobs the chip exposes. Reproduce the inverted-U retention curve ON SILICON with MEASURED
    energy. This is the headline: intrinsic device noise as free consolidation, energy moat that STRENGTHENS
    as devices get noisier.
E3. BASELINES (matched-budget, on the same sequential-task suite): OUA (2410.13563), MESU, EWC, Benna-Fusi
    complex synapses, plain replay, and -- critically -- the UNCONDITIONED OU anchor (to prove the
    conditioning is what buys retention). Metric: retention vs energy/compute; and on BSS-2, retention vs
    measured joules.
E4. (stretch) longer task sequences; a second modality; the noise-optimum vs task-similarity relationship.

PRE-REGISTERED KILL CONDITIONS (commit to PLAN.md BEFORE running; and COMMIT that file before results)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
K1. GATE F fails (no inverted-U; noise does not help retention beyond the unconditioned anchor) -> the
    mechanism is OUA/MESU/EWC renamed; honest negative, project dead.
K2. The inverted-U appears in sim but VANISHES on BSS-2 (the real intrinsic noise is the wrong

    generically (adjacent to Kolesnikov-Semenova), not the Doob mechanism; reframe or kill.

CITE-AND-DISTINGUISH (mandatory; the kill-test's list)
------------------------------------------------------
- OUA (arXiv:2410.13563, Oct 2024): the drift's source as an OU learning rule -- CITE AS THE DRIFT'S ORIGIN;
  distinguish: no Doob/h-transform, no barrier/first-passage, names catastrophic forgetting as future work.
- MESU (Nat Commun 2025 / PMC12575836, Eq. 11): the Bayesian-CL anchor verbatim; distinguish: not
  OU/Doob/first-passage; hardware stochastic devices only as a sampling-implementation, no noise-helps claim.
- EWC (Kirkpatrick 2017): the Fisher-anchor ancestor; distinguish: static penalty, no diffusion/conditioning.
- Kolesnikov & Semenova (arXiv:2504.13778, Apr 2025): internal hardware noise -> test-noise RESILIENCE;
  distinguish: single-task robustness, not sequential-task retention, no inverted-U.
- Shaham et al., Stochastic consolidation of lifelong memory (Sci Rep 2022, PMC9339009); distinguish:
  replay/rehearsal, noise-as-harmful, monotonic, no hardware, no per-synapse conditioning.
- Benna-Fusi complex synapses / MESU / probabilistic metaplasticity: the boundary-free-consolidation
  incumbents to beat; distinguish on the noise-as-resource + hardware axis.
- Doob-h-transform generative corpus (Variationally Stable Doob's Matching; Reflected Schrodinger Bridge for
  Constrained Generative Modeling; boundary-crossing-probability diffusions): the ONLY Doob uses found --
  cite to PROVE the synaptic use is absent (this absence is the moat).
