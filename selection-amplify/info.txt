THE ONE-LINE IDEA
-----------------
Real corpora are massively missing-not-at-random (MNAR): curation, filtering, dedup, and collection
systematically CENSOR whole regions of input space. Standard fixes (Heckman correction, IPW, counterfactual-
risk-minimization) only REWEIGHT the observed points -- they can never represent a region with ZERO observed
samples. This swing instead (1) ESTIMATES the selection mechanism s(x)=P(observed|x) to LOCATE the censored
complement the corpus implies but never shows, (2) uses a Doob-h-transform bridge to GENERATE whole new
training units in that region (not impute labels for observed units), and (3) ties the recoverable gain to
SELECTION ENTROPY -- generation buys nothing under ignorable missingness and buys a bounded, s_hat-reliability-
limited COLLAR of advantage under strong MNAR. Leverage grows with scale because bigger curated corpora
censor harder.

WHAT THE KILL-TEST CONFIRMED (the moat) AND WHAT THE HARDENING NARROWED (read first -- hard constraints)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
KILL-TEST (re-derivation gauntlet, all four axes returned CONFIRMED-EMPTY, no shrinkage under pressure):
- No generative/counterfactual augmentation LOCATES the MNAR complement via an ESTIMATED SELECTION FUNCTION
  and GENERATES it at the UNIT level. Reject-inference generative work (1904.11376) and MNAR nets (RMT-Net
  2206.00568, SMART/GAIN) impute the LABEL/entries for units whose FEATURES are already observed
  (feature/label-level), not whole never-observed units. The unit-level-vs-feature-level distinction is
  load-bearing.
- Heckman / IPW / CRM (and 2505.10814 censored-selection distribution regression) only REWEIGHT or correct
  inference -- never fabricate censored units. They are the baseline-to-beat, not prior art.
- ALL Doob-h-transform / guided-diffusion out-of-support generation (DOIT 2602.16198, low-density-region
  exploration 2606.13347, Stein guidance 2507.05482, support-robustness 2605.07220) is driven by rewards /
  classifiers / conditions -- NEVER by an estimated selection propensity. THE UNOCCUPIED MOVE IS: guidance
  signal = estimated selection function. This is the moat.
- No prior states the selection-entropy-gain relationship.

DESIGN-HARDENING (the adversarial critique found NO fatal flaw but closed two silent FALSE-GO paths; both are
non-negotiable or the project ships a false positive):
- FALSE-GO #1, IDENTIFIABILITY HOLE (narrows scope, does not kill). s(x) is NOT pointwise-identified in the
  censored region: there D_obs has zero points, so the density-ratio classifier c(x) is driven entirely by the
  reference density + class prior and s_hat is pure CLASSIFIER OFF-SUPPORT EXTRAPOLATION (a modeling artifact,
  not a measurement) -- exactly where the method acts. THE NARROWING THAT SAVES IT: target only the
  RECOVERABLE COLLAR (censored region reachable by SHORT extrapolation from observed support), gated by
  {density p_hat>=tau} AND {observed-support proximity} AND {LOW classifier epistemic uncertainty}. This
  collar IS the "censored COMBINATIONS of present structure" the scope always fenced to. Make it the honest
  deliverable, not a bug.
- FALSE-GO #2, TRIVIAL CURVE + WEAK CONTROL (re-centers the claim, does not kill). "Gain rises with beta" is
  over-determined: more MNAR mechanically creates more test-shift HEADROOM, so ANY tail-filling generator's
  gain rises -- consistent with the null. And gamma=0 unconditional sampling confounds selection-targeting
  with guidance-induced diversity. THE FIX: the pre-registered discriminator is the METHOD-MINUS-CONTROL SLOPE
  vs selection entropy (NOT the method's own slope), and the control is a PERMUTED / MISDIRECTED-SELECTOR at
  MATCHED guidance strength (NOT unconditional sampling).

THE HONEST CONTRIBUTION IS A CHARACTERIZATION, NOT A CLEAN THEOREM OR A BLOWOUT:
  "Selection-guided generation buys a BOUNDED, s_hat-reliability-limited COLLAR of advantage beyond 1/s_hat
   reweighting, and the method-minus-misdirected-guidance gap TRACKS selection entropy."
Do NOT write "training gain tracks selection entropy" as a theorem (the rise is nearly trivial). Do NOT claim
a blowout over generic augmentation. Do NOT let a SYNTHETIC go/no-go be read as a real-corpus GO.

THE DISTINGUISHING STATEMENT (put a version of this in the intro, verbatim in spirit)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
"Prior MNAR/selection work either reweights estimates (Heckman/IPW/CRM) or imputes labels for units whose
features are already observed (reject inference); prior guided-diffusion out-of-support generation is driven
by classifiers/rewards/conditions. We are the only approach that uses an ESTIMATED SELECTION FUNCTION to
locate a never-observed feature-space region and a Doob bridge to SYNTHESIZE whole new units in the
short-extrapolation collar of that region -- and we show that the advantage of this selection-targeting over
identically-strong MISDIRECTED guidance rises with the corpus's selection entropy. Selection entropy provably
upper-bounds and vanishes with the achievable gain; the discriminating evidence is the method-minus-decoy
gap, not the method's own curve."

THE MECHANISM (three parts, precise)
------------------------------------
1. LOCATE -- s(x)=P(observed|x) by density-ratio-via-classification (Sugiyama 2012 reduction). Train a
   CALIBRATED binary classifier c(x) separating D_obs (label 1) from a REFERENCE pool D_ref covering the full
   support (label 0), built WITHOUT peeking at the true selector: (a) uniform/Gaussian cover of the bounding
   box (2D/tabular), (b) temperature-broadened base-prior samples, or (c) a held-out unlabeled same-domain
   pool not run through the selector (MNIST/CIFAR). s_hat(x) ∝ c/(1-c) up to an x-independent class-balance
   factor; calibrate (isotonic/Platt, held-out), report ECE. Estimate epistemic uncertainty from a >=5-member
   deep ensemble (or MC-dropout variance). IDENTIFIABILITY CAVEAT (load-bearing): s_hat is trustworthy ONLY
   on the collar where both classes overlap; deeper in the complement it is prior-driven extrapolation, so the
   bridge is steered ONLY into the collar (density + proximity + low-uncertainty gate) and NEVER by
   high-variance s_hat.

2. GENERATE -- Doob-h-transform bridge (stated honestly as a BIASED GUIDED APPROXIMATION, not exact
   conditioning). Base VP/EDM diffusion trained on D_obs, score s_theta(x,t). Reverse SDE
   dx=[f - g^2 s_theta]dt + g dW. Soft terminal reward encoding the collar-restricted complement:
     r(x_0) = (1 - s_hat(x_0)) * 1[p_hat(x_0)>=tau] * 1[uncertainty(s_hat,x_0)<=u_max].
   h(x,t)=E_base[r(x_0)|x_t]; conditioned drift ADDS g^2 grad_x log h. THE UNOCCUPIED MOVE: r = estimated
   selection propensity (1 - s_hat). Estimate grad log h by reconstruction/Tweedie guidance
   (h≈r(x_hat_0(x_t,t)), default, ~free) or twisted SMC (Wu 2023 / Trippe FrameDiff, K=64-256, report ESS) for
   headline runs -- BOTH ride the same biased r(x_hat_0) proxy, so state plainly this is a surrogate not the
   exact twist. OFF-MANIFOLD GUARD (mandatory -- maximizing (1-s_hat) alone marches to low-s AND low-p garbage):
   (i) density gate inside the reward; (ii) LOW-t-ONLY guidance (query s_hat/p_hat only near the manifold,
   disable at high noise where x_hat_0 is a blurry off-manifold average); (iii) HARD density veto during
   sampling; (iv) clip guidance-to-score norm ratio <=1; (v) INDEPENDENT validation -- every synthesized batch
   scored by a separate density estimator + nearest-neighbor-in-D_full distance, rejections counted as a
   reported diagnostic.

3. THE HONEST THEOREM -- a BOUND plus a DEMOTED empirical hypothesis. PROVABLE (left end): gain is
   upper-bounded by a covariate-shift term (Ben-David et al.) that vanishes as selection entropy -> 0, so under
   MCAR/MAR there is nothing to recover and any augmentation gain -> 0. NOT PROVABLE (right end): that the gain
   grows and is CAPTURED by our method in proportion to selection entropy -- this is empirical and nearly
   trivial as evidence. Selection-entropy axis = MUTUAL INFORMATION I(O;X) (headline scalar; zero under
   ignorable missingness, upper-bounds recoverable gain), reported with D_comp=KL(p(x|O=0)||p(x)) as the
   complement-specific driver. NOT propensity-spread entropy H(s(X)) alone (a uniform 50% thinning has high
   spread but zero censoring). THE DISCRIMINATING QUANTITY is the METHOD-MINUS-MISDIRECTED-GUIDANCE GAP vs
   I(O;X), never the method's own curve.

EXPERIMENT LADDER (gated; E0 is the afternoon go/no-go for the ESTIMATION step, E2 is the go/no-go CURVE)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
E0. AFTERNOON SANITY (Testbed-A, 2D; the FIRST go/no-go). 8-Gaussian ring / two-moons / pinwheel with a KNOWN
    closed-form p(x). MNAR selector s_beta(x)=sigmoid(-beta*phi(x)), phi a fixed feature that also carries
    label info (e.g. phi=x_1 censors high-x_1 modes). Fit s_hat by density-ratio classifier (>=5-member deep
    ensemble). GATE (REGION-SPLIT -- this is the fix for false-GO #1): Spearman(s_hat,s_beta)>0.7 BOTH overall
    AND on-the-complement-only; small ECE; I(O;X)/D_comp from-s_hat agrees with analytic and moves with beta.
    No generation yet. If s_hat cannot recover a KNOWN 2D selector, or complement-Spearman ~0 while global
    passes (the silent false-GO), STOP and fix estimation before spending anything downstream.
E1. BRIDGE ON 2D (hours). Train base diffusion on D_obs; implement the reconstruction-guidance Doob bridge
    with LOW-t-only guidance, density gate + hard veto, uncertainty gate, guidance-norm cap. Confirm
    synthesized points land in the collar-complement (high D_full density, low s_hat, low uncertainty) and NOT
    off-manifold. GATE: complement-hit-rate and off-manifold-rejection within pre-registered bounds (early
    test of K3 + K3b).
E2. THE GO/NO-GO CURVE (2D, ~1 day). Full beta sweep {0,0.5,1,2,4,8} x >=3 seeds. Downstream classifier on
    D_obs vs D_obs ∪ synthesized; evaluate on frozen full test AND censored slice. Plot THREE curves:
    (i) method gain full, (ii) method gain censored slice, (iii) THE DISCRIMINATING PANEL -- the
    method-minus-control gap on the censored slice vs I(O;X). Run baselines B0-B4 PLUS the sharp permuted-
    selector control AND the diversity-matched B2. Paired Wilcoxon. GATE = left-end-zero confirmed (theorem)
    AND the method-minus-DECOY censored-slice gap RISES with beta and is >0 at high beta with p<0.05. Pass K1
    (rewritten) + K2 (vs the sharp control) here or the project stops.
E3. PERCEPTUAL TESTBED-B (MNIST/CIFAR subpop censoring, 2-3 days). s_beta censors a subpopulation (rotated/
    thick-stroke 8s, or one CIFAR visual mode). Small EDM/DDPM base (~1-2 GPU-hrs); twisted-SMC bridge
    (K=64-256, report ESS) for headline numbers; same three curves + full baseline ladder + sharp control +
    independent density/NN validators + visual off-manifold check. Confirms the mechanism survives structured
    data, not just 2D density.
E4. SCOPE + ROBUSTNESS (1 day). FOREIGN-STRUCTURE PROBE (K4): hold out a genuinely non-recombinable mode;
    the method MUST fail to recover it (positive control for the interpolative-only honesty claim). THE CENTRAL-
    SQUEEZE plot: (method - B3 1/s_hat reweighting) gain AGAINST distance-from-observed-support AND AGAINST
    s_hat epistemic uncertainty -- the thesis "generation buys a bounded collar beyond reweighting, width set
    by s_hat extrapolation reliability." Ablate gamma, tau, guidance-norm cap; inject noise into s_hat and
    watch gain degrade gracefully.
E5. (STRETCH, LIMITS-CHARACTERIZATION ONLY) Real-corpus pilot. One curated-vs-uncurated pair (filtered-vs-raw
    tabular or a small filtered image set) with the COVER-BLIND check (K5). Report honestly as "as good as the
    uncurated pool's coverage," NEVER a clean synthetic-strength claim. The synthetic GO must NOT be read as a
    real-corpus GO. Explicitly not a single point of failure for the paper.

Baseline ladder (E2/E3): B0 D_obs-only (floor); B1 SMOTE/oversampling of low-density D_obs; B2 selection-
AGNOSTIC generative augmentation (gamma=0) + a DIVERSITY-MATCHED B2 (temperature-broadened, tuned to the
guided sampler's entropy so B2 is not beaten merely on spread); B3 1/s_hat importance-reweighting (classical
MNAR fix, no generation -- the central squeeze); B4 oracle trained on D_full (upper bound). THE SHARPEST
CONTROL (false-GO catcher) = PERMUTED/MISDIRECTED-SELECTOR: identical bridge at identical guidance strength,
s_hat replaced by a decoy (fit against a randomly rotated feature, or complement direction negated, or a
label-permuted classifier). Holds the diversity confound FIXED; varies only whether guidance points at the
REAL complement.

PRE-REGISTERED KILL CONDITIONS (commit to PLAN.md BEFORE running; COMMIT that file BEFORE the results commit)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
K1. (REWRITTEN) The censored-region METHOD-MINUS-MISDIRECTED-SELECTOR gap shows no significant positive trend
    across the beta sweep (Spearman(gap,I(O;X)) not sig >0, OR beta=0-vs-beta=max gap fails paired Wilcoxon
    p<0.05 over >=3 seeds). The method's OWN rise is trivial (more MNAR = more headroom); the discriminating
    prediction is the rising method-minus-CONTROL gap.
K2. (CORE FALSIFIER) At high beta the method does not exceed the PERMUTED/MISDIRECTED-SELECTOR control (and the
    diversity-matched B2) on the censored slice (paired Wilcoxon p>=0.05 or effect within noise). Then the
    selection guidance is inert and any gain is generic guidance-induced diversity. Beating gamma=0 B2 ALONE
    is NOT sufficient -- must beat the matched-guidance decoy.
K3. OFF-MANIFOLD GARBAGE. Synthesized complement fails the independent density/NN-distance validator above
    pre-registered thresholds, OR twisted-SMC ESS collapses below a preset floor. Even if downstream accuracy
    moves, do NOT claim success.
K3b. (REGION-SPLIT IDENTIFIABILITY) On-complement Spearman(s_hat,s_beta) not sig >0 while global passes, OR the
    bridge is steered by high-epistemic-uncertainty s_hat (guidance mass outside the low-uncertainty collar).
    Catches the silent false-GO where the pipeline passes on the easy region and hallucinates in the deep
    complement.
K4. SCOPE OVER-CLAIM ON FOREIGN STRUCTURE. If the method appears to recover a genuinely foreign (non-
    recombinable) held-out mode, that is eval leakage/artifact, not capability -- investigate; the
    interpolative-only scope is retracted only with extraordinary evidence.
K5. (REAL-CORPUS ONLY) COVER-BLIND. If the background/reference pool is co-censored on the target region
    (classifier confidence collapses to the class prior there), the real-corpus result is uninterpretable --
    report ONLY the synthetic result, never a real-data claim.

CITE-AND-DISTINGUISH (mandatory; the kill-test's list)
------------------------------------------------------
- Heckman selection correction (1979) / IPW / counterfactual-risk-minimization (Swaminathan-Joachims): REWEIGHT
  the loss, never fabricate censored units. The baseline-to-beat (B3), not prior art.
- Distribution Regression with Censored Selection (arXiv:2505.10814): relaxes Heckman parametrics but still
  corrects estimates/inference under selection; baseline, not prior art.
- Deep Generative Models for Reject Inference in Credit Scoring (arXiv:1904.11376): canonical Heckman-selection
  generative work, but IMPUTES the OUTCOME/label for existing rejected applicants whose FEATURES are observed;
  does not generate whole new units in a never-observed feature region. The unit-level-vs-feature-level
  distinction is load-bearing -- cite and draw it explicitly.
- RMT-Net (arXiv:2206.00568) and SMART/GAIN (PMC12041391): model MNAR selection but are reject-AWARE
  correction / feature-level imputation, not unit-level generation of the complement.
- Amortized Bayesian Inference for selection bias (arXiv:2604.18319): embeds selection into a simulator but
  CORRECTS posterior inference (calibrated posteriors); emits no censored samples as data, no bridge, no
  entropy relationship.
- Bias-Corrected Data Synthesis for Imbalanced Learning (arXiv:2510.26046): nearest-sounding "bridge +
  complement," but on inspection distribution-matching within the EXISTING minority class with an explicit
  correction term -- no estimated selection model, no Doob bridge, no entropy relationship. Cite to PREEMPT
  reviewer pattern-matching (this is the chief presentational risk).
- Out-of-support / low-density guided diffusion (arXiv:2605.07220 support-robustness under guidance,
  2507.05482 Stein guidance, 2606.13347 low-density-region exploration; DOIT/training-free Doob 2602.16198,
  infinite-dim Doob 2602.06621): steer generation into low-density/out-of-support regions but driven by
  classifiers/rewards/conditions, NEVER an estimated selection propensity. The nearest neighbor on the
  GENERATION side -- adjacent-distinguishable; the distinguishing axis is "guidance = estimated selection
  function."

LIMITATIONS TO DISCLOSE (verbatim in spirit -- these ARE the honesty of the paper)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- IDENTIFIABILITY: s(x)=P(observed|x) is NOT pointwise-identified in the censored region; s_hat there is
  classifier off-support extrapolation, not a measurement. The recoverable deliverable is the observed-support
  COLLAR (density + uncertainty-gated), i.e. censored COMBINATIONS of present structure -- not arbitrary
  deep-complement structure.
- THE CENTRAL SQUEEZE: where generation provably beats 1/s_hat reweighting (B3) is beyond the collar, exactly
  where s_hat is least reliable; where s_hat is reliable, B3 nearly matches. The contribution is a BOUNDED
  collar of advantage whose width is set by s_hat extrapolation reliability -- own this as the central finding.
- THE THEOREM is a BOUND + EMPIRICAL HYPOTHESIS: only the left end (gain->0 as selection entropy->0) is proven;
  the rise is empirical AND nearly trivial, so do NOT claim "gain tracks selection entropy" as a theorem. The
  discriminating evidence is the method-minus-misdirected-guidance gap.
- THE DOOB BRIDGE IS APPROXIMATE: reconstruction guidance and twisted SMC both ride the biased r(x_hat_0)
  proxy; described as "guided generation toward an estimated-low-selection collar," not exact Doob
  conditioning.
- SCOPE FENCE: recovers only structure on the manifold spanned by the observed data (interpolative, censored
  combinations); does NOT generate foreign structure. K4 is a positive control that the method MUST fail on
  genuinely non-recombinable modes.
- FEASIBILITY/REALISM GAP: the cheap decisive experiment is SYNTHETIC and its advantage partly rests on a
  full-support reference cover -- a controlled affordance NOT available on real corpora. The real-corpus rung
  (E5) is assumption-gated (K5) and limits-characterization only.
