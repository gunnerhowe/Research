THE ONE-LINE IDEA
-----------------
A latent/continuous chain-of-thought (Coconut-family) reasons by feeding its last hidden state back as
the next input embedding, producing a trajectory of continuous "thought" vectors c_1..c_n. That trajectory
is literally a discrete-time dynamical system c_{t+1}=F(c_t). CLAIM: the interpretable structure of the
reasoning -- the high-influence "anchor" thoughts and the branch points -- is PREDICTED (not merely
observed) by the dynamical invariants of F: anchor thoughts <-> dominant/slow modes of the local Jacobian
(or the estimated Koopman/transfer operator along the orbit); branch points <-> transient-expansion /
non-normal-amplification directions. Validate against ProsQA's KNOWN ground-truth reachability DAG with a
linear-chain null control, and -- MANDATORY -- cross-check CAUSALLY so the spectral objects are shown to be
mechanistic, not epiphenomenal.

CITE-AND-DISTINGUISH (mandatory, in the paper's related work)
-------------------------------------------------------------
- Fernando & Guitchounts, arXiv:2605.14258 (Jacobian spectra, non-normality, spectral gradients OF the
  transformer residual stream): SAME TOOLKIT, DIFFERENT DYNAMICAL OBJECT -- depth-as-time on standard
  forward passes vs your thought-step AR recurrence; and it does NOT touch reasoning/CoT/Coconut. This is
  your single most important tools-overlap distinction and the template to adapt.
- RKSP, arXiv:2602.22988 (whitened-DMD Koopman over LAYER-wise residual snapshots, near-unit-spectral-mass
  diagnostic for TRAINING INSTABILITY): depth-axis + training-dynamics goal, not interpretability of reasoning.
  Borrow the near-unit-spectral-mass idea; distinguish the axis + goal.
- Aswal et al., arXiv:2606.12689 (causal-geometric Coconut/CODI): they MEASURE that low-rank directions carry
  causal load and grow structured; you PREDICT which directions/steps from spectral invariants and EXPLAIN
  why -- and you adopt their control+causal discipline. (Also the armed trap above.)
- Li et al., arXiv:2602.08783 (SCM + step-wise do-interventions on latent CoT): establishes anchors exist
  causally AND that step effects are "routed/non-local" (a substantive threat to a naive local-Jacobian
  story -- see K3). They establish the phenomenon; you supply the dynamical mechanism.
- Carson & Reisizadeh, arXiv:2506.04374 ("A Statistical Physics of Language Model Reasoning"): SDE
  dh=mu(h)dt+B(h)dW at the SENTENCE/token level, PCA(40)+GMM regimes. Closest "reasoning-as-dynamical-system"
  framing but discrete tokens (not latent thoughts), no Jacobian/Koopman/spectra, no DAG. Distinguish:
  deterministic latent spectral operator + DAG validation.
- Sun et al., arXiv:2604.05655 ("LLM Reasoning as Trajectories", discrete CoT, step-specific subspace
  geometry, mid-trajectory correctness ROC-AUC 0.87): discrete-CoT subspace geometry, no spectral/dynamical
  machinery; source of validation metrics (mid-trajectory correctness).
- Attractor/equilibrium reasoner ARCHITECTURES (2605.12466, 2605.21488, 2606.18206, 2605.18820): they BUILD
  fixed-point dynamics; you ANALYZE emergent dynamics of trajectories that already exist. Cite to show you
  know the conversation; distinguish design-vs-analysis.
- NOTE (phantom scoop, do NOT be spooked): a web summary claimed ReLaX (arXiv:2512.07558) "applies Koopman/DMD
  to reasoning trajectories" -- FALSE; the PDF has zero Koopman/DMD (it is an RL latent-exploration training
  method). A search-summarizer hallucination. Verified not a scoop.

THE DISTINGUISHING STATEMENT (put a version in the intro)
--------------------------------------------------------
"Prior work establishes that latent reasoning has heterogeneous, causally-routed 'anchor' structure and that
low-rank directions carry its causal load; and separately applies Koopman/Jacobian-spectral tools to the
transformer's DEPTH axis. We are the first to characterize the latent-reasoning AUTOREGRESSIVE map
c_{t+1}=F(c_t) spectrally, and to show that its dynamical invariants -- dominant/slow modes and non-normal
transient-expansion directions -- PREDICT the anchor thoughts and branch points, validated against a
ground-truth reasoning DAG with a linear-chain null control and confirmed by causal ablation."

EXPERIMENT LADDER (gated; E0 is a ONE-WEEK go/no-go)
---------------------------------------------------
E0. WEEK-1 GO/NO-GO (spectral prediction + the make-or-break null control). NO TRAINING NEEDED.
    - Download bmarti44/coconut-curriculum-checkpoints (HuggingFace): base openai-community/gpt2 (124M),
      ProsQA, 7-stage curriculum, variants M1 (CoT baseline), M2 (Coconut hidden-state recycling),
      M3 (pause-token single-pass), M4 (pause-token 6-pass). M3/M4 are BUILT-IN null controls -- they match
      Coconut accuracy (96.6 vs 97.0) WITHOUT the latent recurrence, i.e. same behavior, different mechanism.
    - Load ProsQA from facebookresearch/coconut (data/prosqa_*.json). ProsQA = synthetic graph-reachability
      with a KNOWN reasoning DAG; branch nodes = out-degree > 1; linear-chain instances = the natural null.
    - Per problem, run M2, capture latent thoughts c_1..c_n (c_t in R^768). At each step compute
      J_t = dc_{t+1}/dc_t (768x768, torch.func.jacrev, seconds on a 3080); record eigenspectrum, spectral
      radius, Henrici non-normality (||J||_F^2 - sum|lambda_i|^2), top-SVD expansion direction.
    - HYPOTHESIS TESTS vs ground truth: (a) do high-transient-expansion / high-non-normality steps coincide
      with ProsQA BRANCH nodes (out-degree>1)? (b) do dominant/slow-mode steps coincide with causally-high-
      influence ANCHOR thoughts (measured by YOUR OWN step-ablation, cross-referenced to the leverage pattern
      in 2602.08783)? Report AUROC/precision-recall of the spectral predictor against the DAG labels.
    - NULL CONTROL (make-or-break): repeat on (i) linear-chain single-path ProsQA instances and (ii) the
      pause-token M3/M4 models. The thesis REQUIRES the branching/expansion signatures to VANISH there. If
      M3/M4 (same accuracy) show the SAME spectral structure, the spectral objects are epiphenomenal and
      2606.12689 pre-refutes you -> K1 fires, project dead. You learn this in week one.
E1. CAUSAL VALIDATION (mandatory gate, the 2606.12689 discipline). Intervene on the spectrally-identified
    anchor directions / branch steps (ablate, patch, or perturb along the dominant mode) and show the
    predicted downstream reasoning change; show random/other-direction interventions do not. This converts
    "spectral objects correlate with anchors" into "spectral objects ARE mechanistic." Without E1 the paper
    is the descriptive/incremental version -- do not stop before it.
E2. LOCAL-JACOBIAN vs ROUTED dynamics (the 2602.08783 threat). If the local step-to-step Jacobian misses the
    non-local/routed effects, escalate to a full Koopman / transfer-operator estimate over the ORBIT
    (delay-embedding + EDMD/DMD on the c_1..c_n sequence, Ruelle-Pollicott resonance estimate) rather than
    local linearization. THIS is where your expertise earns its keep and where the moat is deepest.
E3. SCALE / ROBUSTNESS (as budget allows). More ProsQA problems (>=5 seeds of the analysis pipeline for CIs);
    the M1 CoT baseline for contrast; if feasible a second latent-reasoning model (CODI) to show the lens is
    not Coconut-specific. Honest about the GPT-2-124M/toy-ProsQA scope.

PRE-REGISTERED KILL CONDITIONS (commit to PLAN.md BEFORE results; commit that file BEFORE the results commit)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
K1. NULL-CONTROL FAILURE: the pause-token M3/M4 models (or linear-chain instances) show the SAME
    branch/expansion spectral structure as Coconut -> the spectral objects are epiphenomenal, 2606.12689
    pre-refutes -> honest negative, project dead (or reframe as "spectral structure is a property of the
    architecture, not the reasoning" -- itself a small honest result).

    downstream change beyond random-direction baselines -> the lens is descriptive-only, not mechanistic ->
    demote to incremental; do not overclaim "explains".

    structure because reasoning routes through attention in a way the state-to-state operator misses ->
    report the honest limitation of the dynamical-systems lens for routed computation.
